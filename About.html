HAIKU - THE ASIAN KITCHEN

It took one woman's will and desire to forever transform the pan asian foodscape of Hyderabad. A first of its kind standalone pan asian restaurant offering a delightfully nuanced menu serving authentic delicacies from the far east. Haiku opened its doors with a solemn pledge to provide wholesome and nostalgic Pan Asian cuisine for the discerning or most ardent foodie in the city of Hyderabad.

THE REASON WE EXIST

TEAM HAIKU

With service that goes beyond just waiting tables but rather spent in elevating guest experience on every visit. Our staff is the real strength behind our success in the hospitality world - an earnest team of joyful and enthusiastic service providers. Upholding the ethos of a remarkable and trustworthy pan asian food destination- our chefs in the Haiku kitchen always strive for diligence and consistency in cuisine.

VISION

With a goal to focus on authentic asian flavours coupled with exceptional service in a safe and comfortable setting. Haiku has a no liquor policy and continues to provide a multiverse asian food experience in our restaurants or in the comfort of your home. Our Haiku of love with Hyderabad will never cease to end thanks to our beloved guests that support our growth every time they choose to dine with us

OUR FOUNDER

SUMA CHEREDDI

An enterprising engineer turned entrepreneur, Suma wanted to bring an elevated dining experience in the city of Hyderabad. With her degree from JNTU and after having created a mark in the city with her all day cafe Kaficko – the group launched its most renowned and awarded Pan-Asian restaurant – Haiku in 2016. Since then Haiku has been one of the most sought after Pan-Asian restaurants in town catering to a diverse clientele. Followed by Nove – The Italian Kitchen that has gained recognition for serving authentic Italian cuisine in the city of Hyderabad! With two large scale restaurants now underway in Kokapet, Hyderabad, Sensei Hospitality is always finding ways to create unparalleled dining experiences and has plans to move to other cities in the future.
